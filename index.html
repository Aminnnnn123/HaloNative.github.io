Our Mission

Halo Native’s mission revolves around primarily bringing each of our students to a fluent Romanian-speaking level. At the same time, we want to offer them a modern and interesting way of doing so: by showing them the beauty of their country through the provision of cultural facts about Romania. This way, they will not only learn how to speak and write in Romanian but will also improve their general knowledge about their own country.
